Welcome

I'm primarily a mathematician and a data scientist. However I have applied those skills in a number of different fields. I hold PhDs in physics and economics, and I am currently a Chancellor's fellow at the Usher Institute, The University of Edinburgh. My work is primarily in health data science, epidemiology, cryptography and statistics.

This page has some information about me, my work, publications, interests, and a blog where you can read my thoughts on topics related to science, data, and anything else that takes my fancy.
